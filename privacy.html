SnapBalance

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

SnapBalance ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address provided during registration, used for account identification and recovery.
  • Profile Data: Name, age, gender, height, weight, activity level, and dietary preferences entered during onboarding to calculate personalized nutrition targets.
  • Food Logs: Meal names, nutrition data, meal photos, and timestamps that you log through photo scanning, barcode scanning, voice input, text input, or manual search.
  • Weight Entries: Weight data you manually enter for progress tracking.
  • Water Intake: Daily water consumption data you log.
  • Recipes: Saved recipes and meal preferences.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Apple Health Data: With your explicit permission, we read workout data, step counts, active calories, walking/running distance, and weight from Apple Health. We do not write data to Apple Health.
  • Camera and Photo Access: Used solely to photograph meals for AI nutrition analysis and to scan barcodes and restaurant menus. Photos are stored locally on your device.
  • Location Data: With your permission, approximate location is used to provide region-relevant food recommendations. Location data is not stored or transmitted to third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini AI: Meal photos are sent to Google's Gemini API for nutrition analysis. Photos are processed and not stored by Google beyond the API request. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenFoodFacts: Barcode data is sent to OpenFoodFacts to retrieve product nutrition information. See OpenFoodFacts Privacy Policy.
  • USDA FoodData Central: Food search queries are sent to the USDA API to retrieve nutrition data. No personal information is transmitted.
  • Unsplash: Food names are used to fetch representative meal images. No personal information is transmitted. See Unsplash Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription purchases. See RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide personalized nutrition targets based on your profile
  • Analyze food photos and return nutrition estimates
  • Track your daily calorie, macronutrient, and micronutrient intake
  • Display your workout and activity data from Apple Health
  • Adapt your calorie goals based on weight trends
  • Generate personalized meal recommendations
  • Process subscription purchases
  • Send account recovery information to your email (if requested)

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All meal logs, profile data, weight entries, water intake, and food photos are stored locally on your device. Your data remains on your device.
  • No Cloud Database: We do not maintain a cloud database of your personal health or nutrition data.
  • Email: Your email address may be stored for account identification purposes.
  • Encryption: Data transmitted to third-party APIs uses HTTPS encryption.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only with the third-party services listed in Section 1 for the sole purpose of providing app functionality.

5. Apple Health Data

We request read-only access to the following Apple Health data types:

  • Active Energy Burned
  • Basal Energy Burned
  • Workouts
  • Step Count
  • Walking/Running Distance
  • Heart Rate
  • Weight

This data is used exclusively to display your activity information within the app. We do not store Apple Health data on any external server or share it with third parties.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Delete Your Data: You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in your device settings.
  • Revoke Permissions: You can revoke camera, location, or Apple Health permissions at any time through your device Settings.
  • Manage Subscription: You can cancel your subscription through your Apple ID settings at any time.
  • Email: Contact us to request deletion of your email from our records.

7. Children's Privacy

SnapBalance is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
